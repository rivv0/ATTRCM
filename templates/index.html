What is ATTR-CM?

Amyloid Transthyretin Cardiomyopathy (ATTR-CM) is a life-threatening, progressive disease characterized by the buildup of amyloid fibrils composed of misfolded transthyretin protein in the heart.

Early diagnosis is crucial for effective treatment and improved patient outcomes.
